Our Story

We Believe Everyone Deserves
Financial Freedom

FinTrack was born from a simple idea: managing money shouldn't require a finance degree. We built a tool that makes personal finance accessible, beautiful, and even enjoyable.

Our Journey

From a Spreadsheet
to a Movement

FinTrack started in 2021 when our founders — two software engineers drowning in student loans and credit card debt — realized there was no tool that made personal finance feel approachable.

What began as a shared Google spreadsheet evolved into a full-featured platform used by over 10,000 people across 50+ countries. Our goal has always been the same: help real people make real financial progress.
